Esco Proud

I grew up in Escondido and remember gathering for ice cream in Escondido after softball games or for birthday celebrations at places like Swenson's or Farrell's Ice Cream. I have fond memories of those Escondido landmarks and hope that EscoGelato becomes the kind of place where both children and adults create memorable moments of their own. Whether you live here or you're in town visiting San Diego's Safari Park be sure to stop by our place for a relaxing treat.
